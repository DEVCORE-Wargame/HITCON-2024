Your Ideas, Organized / 理順您的靈感

Our Mission

We believe that ideas are powerful, and capturing them should be effortless. Our online Markdown notes app is designed to provide you with a seamless, distraction-free environment to jot down your thoughts, organize your projects, and bring your creative ideas to life. Whether you're a student, a developer, or a creative professional, our platform is here to empower your productivity.

我們相信，想法具有強大的力量，而捕捉想法應該是輕而易舉的事。我們的線上 Markdown 筆記軟體旨在為您提供一個無縫且無干擾的環境，讓您能輕鬆記錄想法、組織項目並將創意付諸實現。無論您是學生、開發者還是創意專業人士，我們的平臺都將為您的生產力賦能。

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone has access to tools that make idea development simple and enjoyable. By combining the power of Markdown with intuitive design, we aim to redefine how people create and manage content online. Our goal is to create a space where your ideas can grow and evolve without barriers.

我們期待一個人人都能輕鬆使用工具來開發想法的世界。通過將 Markdown 的強大功能與直觀設計相結合，我們致力於重新定義人們在線創建和管理內容的方式。我們的目標是創造一個無障礙的空間，讓您的想法得以自由成長和演變。

Why Choose Us

Our app is built with you in mind. It’s fast, reliable, and accessible from any device. With powerful features like real-time collaboration, version history, and easy export options, we make sure that your notes are always safe, organized, and available whenever you need them. We also value your privacy — your data is not encrypted, but we also don't look at it, after all, it's your privacy, believe it or not, ask our non-existent legal advisor.

我們的應用程式是以您為中心打造的。它速度快、可靠，且可從任何裝置訪問。我們提供新增文件、即時預覽、隱私設定等強大功能，確保您的筆記始終安全、有序，並且隨時可用。我們也重視您的隱私——您的資料未經過安全加密，但我們也不會去看，畢竟這是您的隱私，信不信隨你，不信就問我們不存在的法律顧問。
